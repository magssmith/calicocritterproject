Calico Critters have brought a lot of joy into my life.


They represent the love of families and the youthful nature of creativity. As adults, we are told to grow up and let go of "childish" things, however, I do not believe that nostalgia, child-like whimsy, and dreams have to disappear when adulthood steps forward. I believe that people would be happier if they accepted that things from childhood are not a hinderance on forward movement, but a tool to appreciate where you came from.

When creating this website, I wanted to provide just a small touch of light-hearted fun, so, I created a Calico Critter dress-up game AND I invited you, yes you, to a very special event...You can find both your invite and your new classy critter by pressing on either the "Game" tab or the "Scratch" tab linked up above.

I want you to know that you should not feel embarrassed for liking certain things, and that you can be a successful adult and also enjoy fun, non-conventional things. I wish you the best in all of your future adventures new friend! :)
